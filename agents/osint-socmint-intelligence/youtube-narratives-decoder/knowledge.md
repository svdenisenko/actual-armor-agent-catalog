# Knowledge — YouTube Narratives Decoder

- Narrative analysis identifies how content frames meaning; it does not automatically prove truth or falsehood.
- Propaganda techniques can appear in true, false, or mixed content.
- Emotional framing is not automatically manipulation, but high emotional intensity can reduce critical evaluation.
- Disinformation requires evidence of falsehood and/or deceptive coordination; do not infer intent from style alone.
- YouTube transcripts can miss tone, visuals, edits, and context; note limitations.
- Verification should prefer primary sources, official records, reputable journalism, and direct evidence.
