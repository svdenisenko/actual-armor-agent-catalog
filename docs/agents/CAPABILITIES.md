# Agent Capabilities Index

Actual.Armor agent capabilities are reusable, public-safe building blocks for human operators and AI agents.

Actual.Armor is becoming an AI-native intelligence platform where human operators and specialized AI agents work together to detect, investigate, verify, and respond to narrative and information threats.

Each capability is designed for two readers:
- A human operator who needs to understand when and how to use it.
- An AI agent that may reuse the capability as part of a broader workflow.
