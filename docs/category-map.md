# Category Map

This map defines the marketplace taxonomy used by Product, CMO, and QA.

## 1) Narrative Intelligence & Response
Agents focused on narrative analysis, response planning, framing, and rapid rebuttal support.

## 2) OSINT / SOCMINT Intelligence
Agents focused on open-source and social-channel monitoring, signal extraction, and cross-source validation.

## 3) Strategic Intelligence
### 3a) Due Diligence
Pre-engagement and background intelligence for opportunities, counterparties, and strategic decisions.

### 3b) Corporate & Executive Risk
Brand, executive, and organization-centered exposure intelligence and reputation risk mapping.

### 3c) Market / Competitive Intelligence
Competitor positioning, market narrative movement, and strategic landscape interpretation.

## 4) Risk Intelligence
Threat, exposure, and risk-oriented analysis beyond pure narrative monitoring.

## 5) Media / Content Intelligence
Content-specific decomposition, media channel interpretation, and messaging trend analysis.

## 6) Productized Agent Suites
Multi-agent bundles or bundled workflows packaged as a productized offering.

## 7) Education / Sensei Agents
Training, enablement, and educational support agents.

## 8) Sales / Growth Intelligence
Go-to-market, lead intelligence, messaging optimization, and growth-focused analysis.

## 9) Uncategorized / Needs Review
Temporary holding category for agents lacking enough metadata for confident assignment.
