# Executive Authority Auditor

## Purpose
Executive Authority Auditor helps users evaluate public executive credibility and claim consistency using public, attributable sources.

## Target users
- founders
- investors
- analysts
- due diligence teams
- partnership teams
- executive search teams
- media / research teams

## Current readiness
- Catalog status: `full_spec_draft`
- QA status: `not_tested`
- Promotion status: `not_promotion_ready`

## Visibility
This agent is `public_limited` because executive/person-identity diligence can involve sensitive claims, source ambiguity, and elevated misuse risk; strict safety controls are required.

## Example use cases
- Validate whether a claimed executive title is supported by current company pages and professional profiles.
- Compare advisor/board claims against visible organization, conference, and publication records.
- Flag gaps between claimed authority and attributable public evidence without making unsupported accusations.
